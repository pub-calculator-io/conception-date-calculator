This conception calculator calculates your conception date based on the date of your last period, your ultrasound date, or your due date.

[https://www.calculator.io/conception-date-calculator/](https://www.calculator.io/conception-date-calculator/)

== Usage ==

Add the Conception Date Calculator shortcode to your page, post or sidebar:

`[ci_conception_date_calculator]`

Add the following code to your template where you would like the Conception Date Calculator to appear:

`<?php display_ci_conception_date_calculator(); ?>`

== Installation ==

1. Upload the Conception Date Calculator /ci_conception_date_calculator/ folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the Conception Date Calculator plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress.
